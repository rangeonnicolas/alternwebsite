 Openalternativa

OpenAlternativa is a non-profit collaborative database proposing alternatives for a more ecological and ethical way of consumption.

 What we do

As we all know, consumption habits can have impacts on issues such as Climate Justice, Sustainable Development, Health, Labor Conditions or Financial Ethics. Well... you know this. Now, OpenAlternativa gives you the alternative solution!

 Get involved !

Go, surf on the web and find information about how the products we use in our everyday life impact the world both in an environmental and a social way. Find and add ecological and ethical alternatives to your and our way of consuming!

Every information in this database should be linked to an information source (NGO reports, investigative journalism, scientific papers, newspapers, etc.)
