OpenAlternativa

OpenAlternativa is a collaborative database of consumption alternatives. As we all know, consumption habit can have som impacts on Climate, Health, Working conditions, etc. Get (or post) pieces of information about these impacts, and find (or add) alternatives to your way of consuming! Each information in this database may be linked to an information source (investigative journalism, scientific papers, newspapers, etc.)

OpenAlternativa will be available soon. If you are interested in the project, and/or if you want to contribute to it, please enter your email here!
